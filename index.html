What is this?

This is just a game concept that unites two awesome games into one: Pong, one of the first arcade games ever created, and Portal, one of the most innovative games ever created.

How do I play it?

  1. Get a friend, since this is a game for two (or you can play against yourself, which is fun too)
  2. The goal is to score a point by having the ball cross over your opponent's racket
  3. Hit space to start. Player 1 uses Q to move the racket upward and A for downward. Player 2, respectively, uses P and L
  4. When the ball is moving right and goes through the blue portal, it comes out of the orange portal and vice-versa.
  5. Have fun.

Is that it?

Yes. As I said, this is only a concept. If you have any interesting ideas to make it better, you can either share them with me or create your own version. The code is available at GitHub.
